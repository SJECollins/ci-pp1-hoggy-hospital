Help A Hog

Help Us Help Hedgehogs

Interested in helping the Hoggy Hospital's patients? Thank you! We are always in need of any help that members of the public are willing to give. Whether it's donating to the hospital, sponsoring one of our longterm residents, or volunteering your time and energy to one of our fundraising campaigns, we are grateful!

As a small wildlife charity, our resources are stretched thin and every donation goes directly to the animals in our care. We could not continue our work without your help.

How You Can Help

We don't just accept financial donations, though they're very much appreciated! Newspapers, puppy-pads, towels - let us know what you have to spare and we'll let you know if we can use it.

Unfortunately, not all of our patients can be safely released after treatment. Contact us to sponsor one of our longterm residents and we'll keep you up to date on how they're doing.

Particularly passionate about our prickly pals? Want to help fundraising? We are always grateful for people who want to contribute their time to help us. Reach out and tell us how you can help.
